(c) The distance between the electron and the proton in the hydrogen atom is about $5 \times 10^{-11}$ m. What is the magnitude of the (i) electric force, (ii) gravitational force between these two particles. Compare the results. Consider the value of charge of the proton and electron is $1.6 \times 10^{-19}$ C, mass of the electron $m_e = 9.1 \times 10^{-31}$ kg; mass of the proton $m_p = 1.7 \times 10^{-27}$ kg. The gravitational constant $G = 6.7 \times 10^{-11}$ N·m²/kg² and permittivity constant $\varepsilon_0 = 8.854 \times 10^{-12}$ C²/N·m².
