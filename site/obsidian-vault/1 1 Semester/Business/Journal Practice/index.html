    📘 Practice Transactions (1-10):

    1️⃣ Owner started business with \$20,000 in cash. 2️⃣ Purchased furniture for \$3,000 in cash. 3️⃣ Purchased goods worth \$5,000 on credit from Mr. Karim. 4️⃣ Sold goods for \$6,000 in cash. 5️⃣ Sold goods on credit to Mr. Rahim for \$2,000. 6️⃣ Paid rent \$1,200 in cash. 7️⃣ Received commission \$500 in cash. 8️⃣ Paid electricity bill of \$300 in cash. 9️⃣ Owner withdrew \$1,000 for personal use. 🔟 Paid salary to employees \$700 in cash.
