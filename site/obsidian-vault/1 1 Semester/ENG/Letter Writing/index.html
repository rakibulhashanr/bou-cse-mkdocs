    üßæ Full Cover Letter Format (for printed or formal attachment):

    1. Your address (top-right or top-left)
    2. Date
    3. Recipient's name and address (if known)
    4. Subject line (optional in printed letters)
    5. Salutation (Dear Sir/Madam or Dear [Name])
    6. 1st Paragraph ‚Äì Why you're writing, the position you're applying for
    7. 2nd Paragraph ‚Äì Summary of education + experience
    8. 3rd Paragraph ‚Äì Why you're suitable for the job
    9. Closing Paragraph ‚Äì Request for interview or next step
    10. Formal closing (Sincerely / Yours faithfully)
    11. Your full name and signature
